Solar Power Plant

A Solar Power Plant is a facility where large-scale solar energy systems are used to convert sunlight into electricity. This is typically achieved through solar panels that capture sunlight and convert it into electrical power via photovoltaic cells. These plants can generate clean, renewable energy, reducing dependence on traditional fossil fuels.

Solar power plants come in various sizes, from small rooftop installations to large solar farms that can power entire communities. The electricity generated can be stored in batteries or supplied directly to the electrical grid. By promoting clean energy, solar power plants contribute to environmental sustainability and help combat climate change.


Types:
On-Grid

An on-grid solar system, also known as a grid-tied solar system, is a popular choice for solar panel installation in tamilnadu, functions alongside the grid. Accordingly, any excess or shortage of energy can be fed into the grid through net metering. Since they may receive credit for any excess power their system generates and cut their electricity costs, many home customers are choosing on-grid solar systems. Either from the grid or the solar system, you will always have power. They are battery-free.

Off-Grid

The Off-Grid solar system is a battery-based, independent solar system that does not need a utility grid to illuminate your places & it is a complete solar setup with solar panels, solar batteries, and solar inverters, and it is ideal for lighting a home even in a far-off location. The solar system is more prominent as it comes with a power backup. It is also referred to as a standalone solar power system. These systems are completely independent of any power grid. The use of solar power is most prevalent in rural areas where grid electricity is not available or where the cost of electricity is high. When it comes to solar energy, it is best to generate your own power by installing a solar power system.

Solar Water Heater

A Solar Water Heater is a system that uses solar energy to heat water for residential or commercial use. It consists of solar collectors, typically installed on rooftops, which absorb sunlight and convert it into heat. The heated water is then stored in an insulated tank for later use, reducing reliance on conventional energy sources.

Solar water heaters are energy-efficient and environmentally friendly, making them an ideal choice for households and businesses looking to cut down on electricity consumption. By using renewable solar energy, these systems reduce utility bills and carbon emissions, providing a sustainable solution for everyday hot water needs.

Solar Pump

India's agriculture sector is heavily dependent on monsoons for natural irrigation. Pumps are used as an artificial means to provide water for irrigation. Farmers rely on grid electricity or diesel gen-sets to run the pump, which leads to huge delays and economic stress. Hence, for our farmers, an effective irrigation system such as the Solar Water Pump is a great boon. It increases their crop yield by ensuring a reliable and perennial supply of water to their fields.

A solar water pump is an application of photovoltaic technology which converts solar energy into electricity to run the pumping system thereby, replacing erratic grid supply and pollution-causing diesel-powered versions. The solar water pump is powered by solar modules that helps draw surface or ground water out for irrigation.
